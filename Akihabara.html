Akihabara

Akihabara is a shopping center known for its electronics retailers, which range from small kiosks to sprawling shopping malls such as Yodobashi Multimedia Akiba. Dedicated venues for manga, anime, and video games include the Tokyo Anime Center, for exhibitions and souvenirs, and Radio Kaikan with its 10-floor high-rise area for toys, card exchange, and collectibles. Staff dressed as maids or butlers serve tea and dessert at a nearby maid's cafe.
